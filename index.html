Description

Jump is a challenging climbing platformer where you create a line to gain a second jump by touching the line.

Create a line adjust the direction and force the longer the line, the stronger your jump will be, and the goal of Line Jump is to avoid falling off the platforms to reach the top.

While you try to reach the top you will encounter various stages which has its own obstacle like falling rocks on the volcano stage or different mechanics like memorizing platform positions while you switch vision.

How to play.

Create Lines for a second jump to reach the platforms.

Line Jump is for you if

  • You like challenging games.
